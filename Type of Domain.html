Type of Domain

  • Types of domains include Top-Level Domains (TLDs) like .com and .org, which are categorized further into Generic (gTLDs) and Country Code (ccTLDs). Another category is Sponsored (sTLDs) such as .gov or .edu, while other classifications focus on a domain's place in the hierarchy, such as Second-Level and Third-Level

    • Example

  • google.com
  • wikipedia.org
  • amazon.com
  • facebook.com
  • whitehouse.gov
  • harvard.edu
