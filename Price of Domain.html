Price of Domain

  • A domain name generally costs between $10 and $20 per year, but the price varies significantly based on factors like the domain extension, registration period, and whether it's a premium or aftermarket domain. The initial registration price can be lower with introductory offers, but renewal fees may be higher. Premium domain names can cost hundreds or even thousands of dollars.
