Price of Hosting

  • The price of web hosting varies widely depending on the type, from as little as $2-$5 per month for basic shared hosting to $50-$700+ per month for dedicated servers. Factors like server resources (CPU, RAM, storage), traffic, and contract length influence the final cost, with introductory rates often increasing significantly upon renewal.
