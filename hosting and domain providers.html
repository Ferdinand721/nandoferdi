Hosting and Domain Providers

  • Popular hosting and domain providers include Hostinger, GoDaddy, Bluehost, and HostGator, which offer a range of services like shared hosting, VPS, and domain registration. When choosing, consider factors like price, performance, and ease of use, especially if you're a beginner. You can either purchase domains and hosting from the same provider or use different ones for more flexibility.
