About Hosting

  • Hosting is a service that provides the physical storage and infrastructure to make websites and applications accessible on the internet. Its function is to store all the files (like text, images, and code) on powerful, always-on computers called servers, allowing users to access the content from anywhere at any time.

    • Function

  • Stores website files: Hosting providers store all the necessary files for a website on their servers, making them available to visitors.
  • Ensures accessibility: By keeping websites on servers connected to the internet 24/7, hosting ensures that a site is always accessible to users.
  • Provides performance: Hosting services manage the server's resources to ensure your website loads quickly and runs smoothly, which is crucial for user experience.
  • Maintains security: Hosting providers often offer security features, including protection against attacks and regular backups, to keep your website and its data safe.
